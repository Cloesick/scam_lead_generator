## Brand Guidelines (from carterlangfold.com)

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Hex (approx) | Usage |
|------|-------|--------------|-------|
| **Primary** | Dark Navy / Charcoal | `#1a1a2e` or `#2d2d44` | Backgrounds, headers, nav |
| **Accent** | Gold / Amber | `#d4a853` or `#c9a227` | CTAs, highlights, icons |
| **Secondary** | White | `#ffffff` | Text on dark, cards |
| **Text** | Off-white / Light grey | `#f5f5f5` | Body text on dark backgrounds |
| **Subtle** | Muted grey | `#6c6c6c` | Secondary text, borders |

> **Note**: Extract exact hex values from the live site CSS or ask client for brand kit.

### Typography
- **Headings**: Modern sans-serif (likely Inter, Poppins, or similar)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif, good legibility
- **Style**: Professional, premium, trustworthy

### Visual Style
- **Photography**: High-quality home/construction imagery, natural lighting
- **Layout**: Clean, spacious, minimal clutter
- **Tone**: Professional, confident, reassuring (not aggressive or salesy)

### Logo
- Carter Langfold wordmark (request vector/PNG from client)
- Placement: bottom-right on ad images, or top-left on video

### Ad Image Guidelines
- Use dark navy backgrounds with gold accent elements
- White text for headlines
- Gold for CTAs and highlight elements
- Include logo in corner
- Avoid stock-photo look; prefer real project photos if available

## Compliance Reminders (do not skip)

- **No finance language** in any ad (no "monthly payments", "0% finance", "apply for finance")
- **No guaranteed outcomes** ("we will fix your mortgage", "guaranteed valuation increase")
- **No unverified "scam" accusations** unless legally reviewed
- **Landing page must have**: privacy policy, real contact details, consent controls
- **Geo**: England, Wales, Northern Ireland only (exclude Scotland)
