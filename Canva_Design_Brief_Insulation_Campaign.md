## BRAND KIT (Set Up First in Canva)

### Colour Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary Dark** | `#1B2A4A` | Headlines, nav bar, trust elements — conveys authority/legal credibility |
| **Primary Accent** | `#2563EB` | CTA buttons, links, key highlights — professional, trustworthy blue |
| **Warning/Urgency** | `#DC2626` | Damage callouts, urgency banners ("Act now", "Time limited") |
| **Success Green** | `#16A34A` | Checkmarks, eligibility confirmations, positive outcomes |
| **Neutral Light** | `#F8FAFC` | Backgrounds, card surfaces |
| **Neutral Dark** | `#334155` | Body text |
| **Warm Accent** | `#F59E0B` | Highlight badges, star ratings, claim value callouts |

### Fonts

| Element | Font (Canva available) | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| **Headlines** | **Inter** or **DM Sans** | Bold / Extra Bold |
| **Subheadlines** | Inter or DM Sans | Semi Bold |
| **Body** | Inter or DM Sans | Regular |
| **CTA Buttons** | Inter | Bold, ALL CAPS or Title Case |

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional but accessible** — not legal jargon, not tabloid
- **Empathetic** — "We understand this is stressful"
- **Authoritative** — reference UK law, scheme names, surveyor obligations
- **Urgent but not pushy** — "Time limits apply" not "CLAIM NOW!!!"
- **Never promise** specific outcomes — use "you may be entitled", "up to", "typical"

### Visual Style
- Clean, minimal layouts with plenty of whitespace
- Real damage photography > stock images (damp walls, mould, crumbling render, insulation extraction)
- Before/after comparisons where possible
- Icons for process steps (flat, outlined style — Canva's "outlined" icon set works well)
- No clip art, no cartoons, no emojis in ad creatives

## COMPLIANCE REMINDERS

- All ads must comply with **ASA** (Advertising Standards Authority) guidelines
- Avoid guaranteeing specific compensation amounts — use "up to" / "typical"
- If operating as a claims management company → **FCA authorisation** required
- If referring to solicitors → ensure **SRA compliance**
- **GDPR:** Explicit consent for data collection, secure storage, clear privacy policy
- Include disclaimers on all ads and landing pages
- Meta ad policy: Claims/financial services ads may require additional verification
